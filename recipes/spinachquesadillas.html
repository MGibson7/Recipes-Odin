Spinach Quesadillas

HOME

Description

Easy Delicious Spinach Quesadillas ready to serve in 17 minutes! Wow!

Ingredients

  • spinach
  • tortillas
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. In a large nonstick skillet, cook and stir first ingredients until spinach is wilted
  2. Remove from heat; stir in cheeses
  3. cook over medium heat until golden brown 1-2 mins
  4. Cut quesdaillas in half
