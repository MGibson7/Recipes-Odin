Corn Bread

HOME

Description

Easy Delicious Corn Bread ready to serve in 17 minutes! Wow!

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Flour
  • Bread
  • butter

Steps

  1. In a large nonstick skillet, cook and stir first ingredients until spinach is wilted
  2. Remove from heat; stir in butter
  3. cook over medium heat until golden brown 1-2 mins
  4. Cut cornbread in half
